LAMP

UX DESIGN & MOBILE APP DEVELOPMENT

Challenge

After graduation, many students find themselves having to relocate to a different city for their job. It can be difficult to find roommates in a place you're not familiar with, especially with a stranger you may not even meet until you move in together.

Outcome

Our app, LAMP, helps UT students and recent grads connect with potential roommates also from UT who are relocating to similar cities after graduation. This way, students can meet with their potential roomie while they are still at UT, while also finding someone who shares common roots as themselves before moving to a brand new place.
